About

Gong Fu Cha is a community of tea enthusiasts. Our purpose is to celebrate, spread, develop, and illuminate the rich culture and activity that is Gong Fu Cha. We provide a place for social networking between tea enthusiasts, facilitate tea gatherings and meet ups, and provide find information on the what, where, why, when, and how of Gong Fu Cha for people both new and experienced.

We founded Gong Fu Cha Community to facilitate easy access through personal referrals/reviews to quality tea companies, tea ware, tea blogs, and tea itself. Our users build this database; all reviews and locations are user-generated, not automatically pulled from any outside sites.
